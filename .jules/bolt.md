## 2026-03-03 - [Optimize native_capitalize in stdlib/text.rs]
**Learning:** `format!` macro overhead and intermediate allocations (like `.collect::<String>()`) can be significantly slower than directly building a pre-allocated string, especially when called frequently in loops.
**Action:** Replaced `format!` and the temporary `String` collection with a pre-allocated `String::with_capacity(text.len())`, followed by direct `.push()` for the uppercased character(s) and `.push_str()` for the rest of the slice. This reduces heap allocations per capitalization from 2 to 1 and eliminates `format!` overhead.
